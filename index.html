⚙️ Connect to Google Sheets

This app saves your data directly to your Google Drive spreadsheet. You need to deploy a Google Apps Script once. Follow the steps below:

Step 1. Open your spreadsheet → click Extensions → Apps Script
Step 2. Delete the existing code and paste the full contents of Code.gs (included in the download). At the top of Code.gs, replace YOUR_SPREADSHEET_ID_HERE with the ID from your spreadsheet URL: docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/THIS_PART/edit
Step 3. Click Deploy → New deployment. Choose type Web app, execute as Me, who has access: Anyone. Click Deploy and authorise when prompted.
Step 4. Copy the Web app URL and paste it below. Done! ✅
